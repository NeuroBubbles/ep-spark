FAQ

  • Is EP Spark medical advice? No. EP Spark provides general informational/supportive content.
  • How do I report a bug? Email support with your device model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce.
  • How do I request content changes? Email support with the details.
  • How do I request data deletion? If EP Spark stores personal data, email support to request deletion (see Privacy Policy).

Include this in your email

  • Device model (example: iPhone 15 Pro)
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Screenshot/video if possible
Response time target: 2–5 business days.
